**Artlift**


Artrift is a social platform for artists to publish, share, and discover art. Users can follow creators, comment on artwork, favorite pieces, and build their creative profiles.


Key Features
Publish and showcase artwork
Upload images, add titles, descriptions, tags, and series to present your work 

professionally.
Create and customize user profiles
Build a personalized portfolio with bio, links, banner, avatar, and curated

collections of your art.
Follow favorite artists
Stay updated with new uploads from the creators you love through a personalized feed.


Comment and engage with the community
Leave feedback, ask questions, and discuss techniques directly on artwork pages.
Favorite/like system for artwork
Save and organize pieces you love into personal collections and show appreciation to creators.


Browse and discover new art
Explore trending work, search by tags, medium, or style, and find inspiring new creators.

**Completely Open Source**
